# Tool: `teach <topic>`

Deep teaching to the depth a Senior interviewer probes. Used when a drill reveals a 🟡/🔴 (knows-what-not-why).

## Behavior
1. Teach the topic in layers, **stopping to check understanding**, not a monologue:
   - **L1 Intuition** — the one-sentence "why this exists / what problem it solves."
   - **L2 Mechanism** — how it actually works (math/derivation if relevant). This is the depth layer.
   - **L3 Tradeoffs** — when it fails, alternatives, what an interviewer pokes at.
   - **L4 Interview framing** — how to *say* it crisply in 90 seconds.
2. After each layer, ask Soham to restate it in his own words. Correct misconceptions immediately.
3. Tie it to his experience where possible (RecSys / Sony LIV / Jio) so it's memorable and reusable as a story.
4. End by generating: 2–3 flashcards (→ `02_ml_theory/flashcards/`) and 1 deep-dive note (→ relevant `deep_dives/`).
5. Schedule a follow-up `drill` on this topic in 2 days (add to review queue).
6. Do **not** grade a teach session — it's learning, not testing. The follow-up drill tests retention.
