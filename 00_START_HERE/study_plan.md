# 7-Week Study Plan — Senior MLE @ Big Tech + RecSys

> Adaptive. This is the *default* path; the daily `plan` command overrides it based on your scores.
> Principle: **depth over coverage.** Each week you go deep on a few things, not shallow on many.
> Daily target: ~1 coding problem + 1 deep-dive/drill + flashcards. ~1.5–2h/day. Weekly mock on Sundays.

## Phase map
- **Wk 1–2: Foundations & Diagnosis** — rebuild DSA patterns, find the real ML-theory gaps.
- **Wk 3–4: Depth Construction** — turn 🟡 "I know of it" into 🟢 "I can derive/defend it." Your differentiator (RecSys) goes deep.
- **Wk 5–6: System Design & Integration** — full ML system design, RecSys cases, connect everything to your resume.
- **Wk 7: Mock-Heavy Polish** — realistic rounds, behavioral narrative, weak-spot cleanup.

---

### Week 1 — DSA reboot + ML theory diagnostic
- **DSA:** Arrays/Hashing → Two Pointers → Sliding Window → Stack/Queue. 1 problem/day, easy→medium as you pass.
- **ML theory:** diagnostic drills across bias-variance, metrics, optimization → find the 3 weakest, mark for deep-dive.
- **RecSys:** 1 case read — two-tower retrieval (anchor to Sony LIV).
- **Behavioral:** draft 2 STAR stories (SageMaker migration, RecSys impact).
- **Sun mock:** short coding round (calibration).

### Week 2 — DSA patterns + first deep-dives
- **DSA:** Binary Search → Trees/BFS-DFS. Start mediums.
- **ML theory:** deep-dive #1 & #2 on your weakest topics (e.g., XGBoost internals, embeddings).
- **RecSys:** candidate generation vs ranking — the two-stage architecture, cold start.
- **Sun mock:** ML theory round.

### Week 3 — Depth construction I
- **DSA:** Heaps/Intervals → Graphs. + 1 ML-coding (k-means or cosine-sim ranking).
- **ML theory:** deep-dive #3 & #4; start ranking metrics (NDCG, MAP) tied to RecSys eval.
- **System design:** learn the design framework spine; do 1 guided case.
- **Sun mock:** RecSys design round.

### Week 4 — Depth construction II
- **DSA:** Graphs continued + revisit any 🔴 pattern. Speed focus (timed).
- **ML theory:** neural-net internals (backprop/init/norm), embeddings deep-dive.
- **RecSys:** ranking models, position/selection bias, online vs offline eval.
- **Behavioral:** 2 more stories (conflict, a failure + what you learned).
- **Sun mock:** coding (medium, timed).

### Week 5 — ML system design
- **System design:** 3 full cases — e.g., "design Sony LIV recommendations," "design a feed ranking system," "design fraud/anomaly detection" (your anomaly background).
- **MLOps:** convert your pipeline experience into a crisp 5-min architecture story.
- **DSA:** maintenance — 3 problems, mixed, timed.
- **Sun mock:** full ML system design round + debrief.

### Week 6 — GenAI + integration
- **GenAI/LLM:** RAG architecture deep-dive, agent design, LLM eval, inference optimization — close the likely gaps.
- **System design:** 1 GenAI case (e.g., "design the log-analyzer/RCA system" — your Jio project).
- **RecSys:** real-time/online learning, multi-objective ranking.
- **Sun mock:** mixed round (coding + design).

### Week 7 — Mock-heavy polish
- **Daily mocks:** rotate pillars. Tighten weakest from the log.
- **Behavioral:** rehearse all stories out loud; refine to Senior scope (influence, ambiguity, tradeoffs).
- **Final:** re-test every 🔴/🟡 topic; lock the differentiator pitch.

---

## Weekly review ritual (every Sunday)
1. Run `status` → read the trend.
2. Mock in the weakest pillar.
3. Re-plan next week: anything still 🔴/🟡 gets prioritized; 🟢/⭐ goes to maintenance.

> Adjust freely — if a phase is too easy you'll be pushed ahead; too hard and we slow down and re-teach. That's the adaptive part.
